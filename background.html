About Us

United College of Science and Technology (UCST) is a distinguished technical institution committed to empowering minds and sharpening futures through quality, practical, and industry-relevant education. Established to meet the growing demand for skilled professionals, UCST has become a beacon of learning and innovation in Kakamega County.

Strategically located along Kakamega–Shinyalu Road at Muranda Market, UCST provides a serene and accessible environment conducive to academic and professional growth. Our institution is dedicated to nurturing talent across diverse technical and professional fields, ensuring that students gain both theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience.

UCST offers a broad range of accredited courses designed to equip learners with the skills needed to excel in today’s competitive job market. Our dynamic student community benefits from a supportive learning atmosphere where creativity, competence, and ethical leadership are cultivated.

At United College of Science and Technology, we take pride not only in academic excellence but also in holistic development. Our students actively participate and succeed in national innovation, sports, and cultural activities, making UCST a hub for well-rounded education.

Join us on the journey to build your future through practical knowledge, technological skills, and a growth mindset—because at UCST, we are truly Empowering Minds and Sharpening Futures.

We pride ourselves on building character, competence, and creativity among our learners. Our alumni are leaving a positive footprint in various industries across the nation.

At UCST, we are committed to delivering not only academic excellence but also a holistic learning experience. Our students frequently excel in National Research, Science and Innovation exhibitions, Sports, and Drama competitions, making us a centre of all-rounded education.

Join us as we continue to raise future leaders through Empowering Minds,Sharpening Futures.
