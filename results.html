KNEC Examination Results

KNEC Examination Results Portal

Welcome to our KNEC Results page. Here, students and stakeholders can easily access official examination results for various levels. Stay updated and informed by following the instructions below to retrieve your KNEC results quickly and securely.

How to Check Your Results

  • Visit the KNEC Official Results Portal.
  • Enter your Index Number and select the examination type.
  • Click on "Submit" to view your results.

Using SMS

Send your Index Number to 20076 via SMS (e.g., 123456789012).

Important Dates

  • Release of November exam results: January
  • Deadline for result remarking applications: February 15

Remarking Information

  • Submit a request at the institution’s office or via KNEC portal
  • Remarking fee: Ksh 1,000 per paper
  • Response within 30 working days

Download Section

  • Download Result Summary PDF
  • Request for Lost Result Slip

FAQs

  • What if my result is missing? Contact the exam office immediately with your index number.
  • Index number not found? Ensure you entered it correctly, or check back later.
