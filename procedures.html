Application Procedures

Begin your educational journey with us. Follow the steps below to complete your application through our online system or manual process.

Online Application

  1. Create your applicant account using a valid email address
  2. Fill in the online application form with your personal details
  3. Select your preferred course of study from our programs
  4. Upload required documents (scanned copies)
  5. Review your application for accuracy
  6. Submit your application electronically
  7. Make payment through the integrated payment system
  8. Receive confirmation email with application reference number
Start Online Application

Manual Application

  1. Download the application form or collect one from our admissions office
  2. Complete the form using block letters or typewriter
  3. Attach certified copies of all required documents
  4. Include application fee payment proof
  5. Submit completed forms in person or by registered mail
  6. Wait for SMS confirmation of application receipt
  7. Track application status via our student portal
  8. Receive admission letter if successful

General Application Requirements

Academic Documents

  • Secondary school certificate
  • Transcripts and result slips
  • National ID copy
  • Passport photos (2)

Additional Documents

  • Birth certificate
  • Recommendation letters (if required)
  • Sponsorship letter (if applicable)
  • Medical certificate

Application Fees

  • Non-refundable application fee
  • Bank deposit slip or payment confirmation
  • Financial aid application (if applicable)
