Electrical Engineering

Department of Engineering & Technology

About the Course

Our Electrical Engineering programme is designed to equip students with the knowledge and hands-on skills needed in modern electrical systems, power distribution, and automation technologies. The course blends theoretical learning with practical training to prepare graduates for a wide range of career opportunities.

Course Units Include:

  • Electrical Installation & Maintenance
  • Control Systems & Instrumentation
  • Power Electronics
  • Digital Systems & Microcontrollers
  • Renewable Energy Technologies
  • Computer-Aided Electrical Design
  • Industrial Attachment

Career Opportunities

Graduates of Electrical Engineering from JNNP can work in energy companies, manufacturing plants, ICT firms, or become successful entrepreneurs. The curriculum is aligned with current industry demands to ensure graduates are job-ready.
