Computing and Informatics
The Department of Computing and Informatics is dedicated to preparing students for the dynamic world of technology. With modern facilities and experienced instructors, learners are exposed to real-world ICT solutions that enhance creativity, problem-solving, and technical expertise.

  • Database Management Systems
  • Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Computer Networks and Security
  • System Analysis and Design
  • IT Support and Maintenance
  • Mobile App Development
