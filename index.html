United College Of Science And Technology

U.C.S.T is a technical institute under the Ministry of Education, State Department of Vocational training. The institute has a current student population of over 3500 and offers over 50 courses in both technical and business fields ranging from plumbing, hairdressing, tourism, food production to ICT and social work. UCST prides itself in instilling core values to its students.
